# Family Nest — Getting Started

A short guide for your family to sign up, log in, and use the Family Nest.

---

## Sign up / Log in

- **New users:** Go to the app URL and choose **Sign up**. Enter your email and a password. After signing up, you’ll be taken to the dashboard.
- **Returning users:** Choose **Log in** and enter your email and password.
- **Invited by someone?** Use the link in the invite email. Sign up or log in with that email; you’ll be added to the family.

---

## Upload photos

1. Open **Photos** (or **Timeline**).
2. Use **Upload** or **Add photo**.
3. Pick a photo from your device (or take one on mobile).
4. Add a caption and date if you like, then save.
5. Photos show in the gallery and on the dashboard.

---

## Write a journal entry

1. Open **Journal**.
2. Click **New entry**.
3. Choose who the entry is about (you or another family member).
4. Add a title, date, and your story. You can attach photos.
5. Save. The entry appears in the Journal and on the Timeline.

---

## Record a voice memo

1. Open **Voice Memos**.
2. Click **Record** (allow microphone access when asked).
3. Record your message, then stop.
4. Add a title and who it’s from / who it’s for, then save.
5. You can play it back anytime.

---

## Add family members

1. Open **Members**.
2. Click **+ Add member**.
3. Enter their full name, how they’re related to you, and optionally email (for an invite), nickname, birthday, and a profile photo.
4. Save. If you added an email, they’ll get an invite to join.

---

## Need help?

- **Can’t log in?** Use “Forgot password” on the login page to reset your password.
- **Something’s broken or confusing?** Contact the person who set up your Family Nest (they’re the one who invited you or created the family).

---

Enjoy building your family’s story together.
